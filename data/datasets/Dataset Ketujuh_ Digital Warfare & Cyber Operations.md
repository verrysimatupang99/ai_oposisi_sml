**Case Studies: Major Hoax Campaigns in Indonesia**

- **Original Incident**: Quranic reference in political speech, religious interpretation controversy
- **Manipulation Elements**: Video editing, context removal, inflammatory framing, religious sensitivity exploitation
- **Distribution Networks**: WhatsApp groups, Facebook shares, mosque networks, religious leaders
- **Amplification Tactics**: Hashtag campaigns, mass demonstrations, media coverage, political instrumentalization
- **Political Impact**: Electoral defeat, religious polarization, identity politics strengthening, democratic backsliding
- **Lessons Learned**: Religious sensitivity, context importance, rapid response needs, fact-checking limitations

- **False Narrative**: Assault fabrication, victim performance, political sympathy generation
- **Visual Evidence**: Fake injury photos, hospital setting, medical documentation forgery
- **Political Exploitation**: Opposition campaign integration, government criticism, sympathy mobilization
- **Unraveling Process**: Medical examination, evidence contradiction, confession extraction
- **Consequences**: Campaign disruption, credibility damage, legal prosecution, political embarrassment
- **Analysis**: Verification importance, source checking, political desperation, backfire effects

COVID-19 Misinformation (2020-2022)

- **Health Misinformation**: Cure claims, prevention myths, vaccine conspiracy theories, treatment alternatives
- **Political Instrumentalization**: Government criticism, policy resistance, authority undermining
- **Economic Exploitation**: Fake product sales, investment scams, donation fraud, commercial manipulation
- **Social Impact**: Public health risks, vaccine hesitancy, treatment delays, social division
- **Counter-Measures**: Fact-checking initiatives, platform policies, government response, health education
